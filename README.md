# 📘 SOIL TO SKY — India’s Self-Reliant Rise

🚀 A collaborative book project that explores the spirit of Atmanirbhar Bharat through Indian literature, ideas, and real-world inspiration.

---

## 🌟 About the Book

“Soil to Sky: India’s Self-Reliant Rise” is a thoughtfully curated book that highlights the journey of India towards self-reliance. It reflects how literature, innovation, and collective effort contribute to building a strong and independent nation.

The book brings together perspectives on:
- 🌾 Rural development and self-reliance  
- 📚 Influence of Indian literature  
- 💡 Innovation and entrepreneurship  
- 🇮🇳 National growth and sustainability
